What to do?

Stone Arch Bridge

Located in downtown Minneapolis. The bridge spans across the Mississippi river, connecting historic St. Anthony Main with the Guthrie Theatre and Mill City Museum. This is where the couple got engaged!

Walker Art Center

Features contemporary visual and performing arts. Located next to the Minneapolis Sculpture Garden (you know, the big spoon and cherry)!

1750 Hennepin Avenue,
Minneapolis, MN 55403

Minnehaha Falls

Located on the south side of Minneapolis, this is a fantastic park to take in some fresh air. If you get hungry, stop by the Sea Salt cafe just steps away from a beautiful view of the falls.

4801 South Minnehaha Drive,
Minneapolis, MN 55417
